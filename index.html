Understanding ROAS vs ROI in Digital Marketing

ROAS (Return on Ad Spend) measures the gross revenue generated for every dollar spent specifically on advertising. It helps businesses understand the direct effectiveness of their ad creatives and targeting strategies.

ROI (Return on Investment) takes the bigger picture into account. It calculates overall profitability by factoring in all additional costs, such as tool subscriptions, design fees, and agency margins. A high ROAS with a negative ROI means you are making sales but losing money overall.
