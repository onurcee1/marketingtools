Essential Digital Marketing Formulars Explained

Maintaining a highly data-driven approach is the key to scaling digital advertising across channels like Google, Meta, and TikTok. This professional multi-tool simplifies routine math for ad optimization workflows.

CPM (Cost Per Mille)

Formula: (Total Cost / Total Impressions) * 1,000. It gauges the cost-effectiveness of your awareness campaigns per one thousand views.

Conversion Rate

Formula: (Conversions / Total Clicks) * 100. It benchmarks how effectively your landing page turns raw interest into paid conversions.
